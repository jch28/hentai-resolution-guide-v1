# Security and Responsible Disclosure

## Our Commitment

We take security seriously and appreciate responsible disclosure of vulnerabilities.

## Reporting Process

1. Email maintainers with details
2. Allow time for investigation and fix
3. Coordinate public disclosure after fix

## Safe Harbor

We will not pursue legal action against researchers who follow responsible disclosure.

See [rule34.ink/security-policy](https://rule34.ink/ref/anvil?to=SECURITY.md) for our full security policy.
